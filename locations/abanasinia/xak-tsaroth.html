Xak Tsaroth

Xak Tsaroth was once a thriving city on the central eastern coast of Abanasinia. The city was founded by the descendants of the Ut-kiri tribe. The wealthy city was led by merchant lords and home to a Temple of Mishakal.

Important Locations

Great Plaza

The Great Plaza of Xak Tsaroth was laid out before the Royal Palace of Xak Tsaroth, and somewhat survived the ruin of the Cataclysm. It currently is in the ruin of the great city underground.

Court of the Aghar

The Court of the Aghar was the throneroom for the Highbulp in Xak Tsaroth.

Hall of the Ancestors

The Hall of the Ancestors was a place in Xak Tsaroth where the former priests and kings of Xak Tsaroth were buried. The staircase Paths of the Dead ends here. When the Cataclysm hit Xak Tsaroth, this Hall sank into the group along with the rest of the city, and was in ruin by 351 AC.

Royal Palace of Xak Tsaroth

The Royal Palace in Xak Tsaroth survived the Cataclysm somewhat intact, but was one of the buildings that slid down into the cavern where it resides presently. Just at the front of it was the Great Plaza. This was where the black dragon Khisanth kept the Disks of Mishakal.

Roads

Paths of the Dead

The Paths of the Dead was a staircase in Xak Tsaroth that would lead to the Hall of the Ancestors where the former priests and kings of Xak Tsaroth were buried. When the Cataclysm hit Xak Tsaroth, the staircase sank into the group along with the rest of the city, and was in by 351 AC.
