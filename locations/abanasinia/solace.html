Solace

Solace is known for its unique and enchanting architecture, as well as its peaceful, close-knit community. Nestled in the forests of Abanasinia, Solace is a town where the buildings are not built on the ground but instead in the towering branches of ancient vallenwood trees. These massive trees form the heart of the town, with homes, shops, and inns elevated high above the forest floor, connected by sturdy wooden bridges and walkways. This elevated design gives Solace a magical, treehouse-like atmosphere, setting it apart from more traditional settlements in Krynn.

Life in Solace is centered around the vallenwood trees, which provide shelter and a sense of unity for its inhabitants. The most famous of these structures is the Inn of the Last Home, a warm, welcoming place where travelers and locals alike gather for food, drink, and company. The inn is run by Otik Sandath, and it serves as a key location in the Solace, often acting as a meeting place for adventurers and travelers alike. The inn is also famous for its spiced potatoes and other rustic, hearty dishes that embody the simple yet fulfilling life in Solace.

Despite the hardships of the war, Solace remains a symbol of hope and resilience. Its people, though tested by war and hardship, embody the spirit of perseverance and community. The town’s rustic charm, its towering vallenwood trees, and the warmth of the Inn of the Last Home make Solace a place that resonates deeply with the people, serving as a reminder of the simple yet powerful values of home, friendship, and hope in a world torn by conflict.

Important Locations

Blacksmith Shop

The Blacksmith Shop in Solace has always been there as long as the city existed. When Theros Ironfeld decided it was time to settle down and get out of the mercenary life, he moved to Solace and took over the blacksmith shop in 349 AC. The shop's reputation grew as a place to get fine weapons.

Clothier's Street

Clothier's Street is one of the roads in the town of Solace in the realm of Abanasinia. The street is known to be the location of most of the cloth merchants in the entire town.

Gods' Row

Gods' Row is a large road located near the Town Square in the town of Solace, in which temples of many of the gods are located. Along Gods' Row are temples dedicated to the gods of good and neutrality, and there are even some temples dedicated to the dark gods, albeit most of them, such as the temple of Chemosh have long since been abandoned.

Magi Alley

Magi Alley is one of the roads in the town of Solace in the realm of Abanasinia. The street is known to be the location of most of the mageware shops in the entire town.

Sword Street

Sword Street is one of the roads in the town of Solace in the realm of Abanasinia. The street is known to be the location of most of the sword dealers in the entire town.

The Trough

The Trough is a tavern of ill repute located in the southernmost reaches of the town of Solace, and is frequented by criminals and all manner of rogues. The tavern boasts a sign of a pig wallowing in swill, and is built on the ground. The building is set between two large Vallenwood Trees and the serving staff of the Trough are well trained to turn a blind eye to any nefarious activity that goes on inside their walls.

The original tavern known as the Trough long since burnt down, and numerous versions of the inn also burnt down. Each time the inn was rebuilt by money supplied by hill dwarves, since it was one of the few establishments in Abanasinia where dwarven spirits were readily available.

Town Square

The Town Square in the town of Solace is set in the absolute center, and is a large cleared area that lies at the intersection of numerous roads. Many merchants setup their market stalls here, and traveling players and musicians also often perform here. The square is a general meeting place for many residents and visitors of Solace.

Inn of the Last Home

The Inn of the Last Home is a tavern in Solace that is famous for its wonderful food and ale, as well as being the meeting place of many adventurers. It has seen several owners and a number of incarnations, but is still one of the coziest and most welcome inns for anyone wandering through Abanasinia.

When Solace was first founded following the Cataclysm, it was only a few short years before a powerful man known as Krale the Strong, decided to build an inn. In these early times of the Age of Despair, desperate marauders would come through the village, prompting the residents of Solace to build their homes in the trees. Krale built the base of the inn from one giant stone, which he raised into a great vallenwood tree through a winch system. Thus suspended and secured forty feet above the ground, he built the rest of the inn into the tree and around his base with the staircase running around the tree. As one would enter the inn they would see a large bean-shaped common room with the only stonework being the fireplace. The floor and ceiling was supported by the vallenwood's smaller limbs. There was only one other exit to the building, and it was a hole in the kitchen that a rope dangled from.

Within a few years, the inn was built. Krale built the inn to last, as he considered the vallenwoods to be the "last homes of Solace". After Krale, the inn saw a number of different owners through the next several hundred years, and by the time a young adventurer named Otik Sandath came along a decade or so before the war, it was already run-down and in bad repair.
