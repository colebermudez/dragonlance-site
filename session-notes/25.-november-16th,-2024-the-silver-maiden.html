1. November 16th, 2024 The Silver Maiden

Bookgreen 28, 360 A.C.

Slowly but surely, Krynn's Vanguard worked their way through the city, taking down key Kingdom targets in Ingrid and Gwendal. With 4 of their 5 major targets taken out, they set their sights on Cornelia.

Along the way, Krynn's Vanguard gained two allies in Arcadius returning after a small detour on his own journey and a friend that showed up with him, Nebula. It's unclear where the two went and how they came to find Krynn's Vanguard again, but that's a conversation for once the fight is over.

Pushing forward and taking out the front guard, Krynn's Vanguard has come face-to-face with the Titanus. Gargantuan constructs of massive power that are as durable as they are powerful. These large magical robots are able to land powerful strikes and even grapple a creature and continuously crush them in their grip. They are accompanied by somewhat powerful mages. Putting a dent in Krynn's Vanguard's forces, Lindhardt and Capsar popped out of existence being banished. They should at least be safe but it does slow them down. This isn't helped when Valphire was knocked unconcious but was thankfully helped by Arcadius swooping in with his Griffin and saving the day. With them closing in, Cornelia is becoming a bit more active. Rather than just throwing out an Ice Storm in their general direction, her attacks are becoming more targeted.

Bound to be a hard fought battle and the prospect of death staring them in them down, can Krynn's Vanguard escape Relgoth unscathed?
