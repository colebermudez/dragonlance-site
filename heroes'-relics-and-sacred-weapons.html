Heroes' Relics and Sacred Weapons

Areadbhar

Weapon (spear), Artifact (requires attunement and Mark/Stone of Blaiddyd)

Description: A long, ornate spear with a blue hue and a distinct blade design.

Properties:

  • +3 to Attack and Damage Rolls: When you attack with this magic spear, you gain a +3 bonus to attack and damage rolls.
  • Azure Strike: Once per day, you can unleash a powerful azure energy strike. All creatures in a 30-foot line must make a DC 17 Dexterity saving throw, taking 8d8 lightning damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
  • Light and Reach: This spear has the light and reach properties.

If the wielder does not bear the correct Mark or possess the correct Stone, their HP maximum is reduced by 10% each time they use the weapon, once per round.

Thunderbrand


Weapon (longsword), Artifact (requires attunement and Mark/Stone of Charon)

Description: A silver sword with a zigzag design and electric motifs.

Properties:

  • +3 to Attack and Damage Rolls: When you attack with this magic longsword, you gain a +3 bonus to attack and damage rolls.
  • Lightning Strikes: Once per short rest, you can cause the blade to crackle with lightning. For 1 minute, your attacks deal an extra 1d8 lightning damage.
  • Storm’s Fury: Once per day, you can cast the spell call lightning (save DC 17) without using a spell slot.

If the wielder does not bear the correct Mark or possess the correct Stone, their HP maximum is reduced by 10% each time they use the weapon, once per round.

Lúin


Weapon (lance), Artifact (requires attunement and Mark/Stone of Daphnel)

Description: A sky-blue lance adorned with intricate designs.

Properties:

  • +3 to Attack and Damage Rolls: When you attack with this magic lance, you gain a +3 bonus to attack and damage rolls.
  • Sky Piercer: Once per day, you can perform a piercing attack that strikes all enemies in a 60-foot line. Each creature in the line must make a DC 16 Dexterity saving throw, taking 6d8 radiant damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
  • Dancer’s Grace: You can use a bonus action to grant yourself advantage on all Dexterity (Acrobatics) checks for 1 hour.

If the wielder does not bear the correct Mark or possess the correct Stone, their HP maximum is reduced by 10% each time they use the weapon, once per round.

Crusher


Weapon (warhammer), Artifact (requires attunement and Mark/Stone of Dominic)

Description: A heavy mace with a blue head and a rugged design.

Properties:

  • +3 to Attack and Damage Rolls: When you attack with this magic mace, you gain a +3 bonus to attack and damage rolls.
  • Earthshatter: Once per day, you can smash the ground and create a shockwave. Each creature within 20 feet of you must make a DC 17 Constitution saving throw, taking 6d8 thunder damage and being knocked prone on a failed save, or half as much damage and not being knocked prone on a successful one.
  • Heavily Armored: While attuned to this weapon, you gain proficiency with heavy armor.

If the wielder does not bear the correct Mark or possess the correct Stone, their HP maximum is reduced by 10% each time they use the weapon, once per round.

 

Aegis Shield


Armor (shield), Artifact (requires attunement and Mark/Stone of Fraldarius)

Description: A large, round shield with a lion motif.

Properties:

  • +3 to AC: While you wield this shield, you gain a +3 bonus to AC.
  • Aegis: You have resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage from nonmagical attacks.
  • Lion’s Roar: Once per day, you can use an action to roar, causing all enemies within 30 feet to make a DC 17 Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, they are frightened for 1 minute.

If the wielder does not bear the correct Mark or possess the correct Stone, their HP maximum is reduced by 10% each time they use the weapon, once per round.

Weapon of the Creator


Weapon (any), Artifact (requires attunement and Mark/Stone of Flames)

Description: A weapon with a segmented blade that can extend and retract.

Properties:

  • +3 to Attack and Damage Rolls: When you attack with this magic weapon, you gain a +3 bonus to attack and damage rolls.
  • Creator's Wrath: Once per day, you can perform a powerful attack. The target must make a DC 18 Dexterity saving throw, taking 10d8 force damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.

Melee properties:

  • Whip Sword: The sword can extend up to 15 feet. When extended, it gains the reach property.

Ranged properties:

  • Always True: No matter the range of this weapon, it will never have any degradation to it's aim. It is also more powerful when it is within a certain distance, giving the wielder better aim.

If the wielder does not bear the correct Mark or possess the correct Stone, their HP maximum is reduced by 10% each time they use the weapon, once per round.
