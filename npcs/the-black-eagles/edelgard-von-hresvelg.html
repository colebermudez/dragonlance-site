Edelgard Von Hresvelg


Born under the shadow of the Cataclysm, a world-shattering event that reshaped the continents of Krynn and the heavens above, Edelgard von Hresvelg is the last scion of the von Hresvelg lineage, rulers of the newly established Abanasinia Empire. The empire was forged in the aftermath of the Cataclysm, seeking to unite the fractured lands of Abanasinia, a region known for its diversity of peoples, including humans, elves, and dwarves, under a single banner to prevent further chaos and suffering.

From a young age, Edelgard was marked by destiny. The mysterious and ancient power of dragon blood ran through her veins, a legacy from an ancestor who had been blessed by a silver dragon. This lineage granted her unparalleled strength and a deep connection to the arcane, manifesting in both her martial prowess and her ability to wield magic—a rarity in the martially inclined von Hresvelg family.

Her early years were fraught with hardship. Her family was embroiled in a silent war against the dark sorcery of the Black Robes, practitioners of evil magic who sought to manipulate the empire's destiny. Edelgard's siblings were casualties of this shadow war, leaving her the sole heir to the throne. Her heart grew hardened by these trials, forging a will of iron and a determination to eradicate the influence of dark magic within her lands and beyond.

Edelgard's rule began in the aftermath of the War of the Lance, a time when the world was still healing from the wounds of conflict and the shadow of Takhisis, the Queen of Darkness, loomed large. Seeing the devastation wrought by unchecked power and ambition, Edelgard vowed to lead the Abanasinia Empire into a new era of prosperity and peace, striving to balance the needs of its diverse inhabitants with the overarching goal of stability and unity.

Motivations:

  • Eradicate Dark Magic: Having witnessed firsthand the destruction and sorrow brought about by the practitioners of dark magic, Edelgard is determined to root out its influence from her empire and the wider world.

  • Unity Through Strength: Believing in the power of a united realm, Edelgard seeks to strengthen the bonds between the various races and cultures within the Abanasinia Empire, fostering cooperation and understanding.

  • Reform and Progress: Understanding the need for change, Edelgard is committed to reforming the empire's outdated structures and laws, encouraging innovation, and embracing the arcane arts as a force for good.

  • Personality Traits: Determined and assertive, Edelgard is a natural leader who is not afraid to challenge the status quo to achieve her vision for a better future.

  • Ideals: Revolution. Edelgard seeks to dismantle the old order and replace it with a meritocracy where one's birth does not dictate their fate.

  • Bonds: She is deeply committed to her cause and her allies, especially those who stand by her as she challenges the very foundations of Fódlan.

  • Flaws: Her single-minded pursuit of her goals can lead to ruthlessness and a willingness to sacrifice anything and anyone for her ideals.

Mark: Mark of Flames; Mark of Seiros
Status: Alive
