Hanneman Stauffen


Hanneman Stauffen is renowned throughout the lands of Ansalon as a sage and scholar dedicated to the study of ancient draconic runes, the mysterious symbols said to be linked directly to the powers of the dragons which once ruled the skies of Krynn. Operating from the vaulted halls of the University of Palanthas, Hanneman is a fixture in the academic community, known for his meticulous research and somewhat obsessive nature when it comes to uncovering the secrets of the past.

Personality Traits

  • Intellectual Curiosity: Hanneman's life is defined by an insatiable curiosity about the world's mysteries, particularly those involving ancient magic and draconic lore.
  • Meticulous: He is known for his meticulous nature, approaching every piece of research with a thorough and sometimes painstaking level of detail.
  • Paternal: To his students and younger colleagues, Hanneman often displays a paternal side, offering guidance and wisdom with a mix of sternness and warmth.

Ideals

  • Knowledge: Hanneman holds a firm belief in the power and importance of knowledge, viewing it as a tool for enlightenment and a safeguard against the repetition of past mistakes.
  • Educational Legacy: He is deeply committed to the idea that educating others is the key to advancing society and preserving peace in a tumultuous world.

Bonds

  • University of Palanthas: Hanneman’s identity and purpose are deeply intertwined with this institution, where he not only conducts his research but also teaches the next generation of scholars.
  • Fellow Researchers: He has built many professional relationships and a few close friendships based on mutual respect for knowledge and discovery.

Flaws

  • Obsession with Research: His obsession with his work can sometimes blind him to the needs and concerns of others around him, leading to strained personal relationships.
  • Overly Critical: Hanneman’s high standards and critical nature can be discouraging to less experienced scholars, sometimes stifling their enthusiasm for learning.

Status: Alive
