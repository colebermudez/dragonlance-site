Lady Rhea


Rhea, serving as the Archbishop of the Church of Seiros, is a figure shrouded in mystery and reverence. She is believed to be a direct descendant of Seiros herself, bearing a profound connection to the celestial dragon that guides their faith. Rhea's leadership is characterized by wisdom, strength, and a deep, unyielding commitment to the preservation of order and the teachings of Seiros.

Under her guidance, the Church has flourished, expanding its influence and solidifying its doctrines. However, Rhea guards many secrets, among them the true nature of the Church's origin and the mystical powers at its disposal. Her primary objective is to maintain harmony and prevent the resurgence of ancient evils that once threatened the world.

in addition to her sacred duties as the leader of the Church of Seiros, Rhea assumes the mantle of Headmistress at the University of Palanthas. This dual role not only exemplifies her dedication to the spread of wisdom and enlightenment but also places her at the confluence of spiritual authority and academic prestige.

Archbishop Rhea's Vision for the University

Under Rhea's stewardship, the University of Palanthas has flourished, becoming a center for learning that attracts scholars, mages, and seekers of knowledge from across Krynn. Her vision extends beyond mere academic excellence; she aims to forge a new generation of leaders, thinkers, and warriors deeply imbued with the values and teachings of the Church of Seiros. Rhea believes that true wisdom lies in understanding the balance between power and humility, between action and contemplation.

Despite its esteemed position, Rhea's leadership and the close ties between the university and the Church of Seiros are not without their critics. Some members of the academic community argue that the Church's influence stifles free inquiry and imposes doctrinal orthodoxy over intellectual exploration. There are also rumors, spoken only in hushed tones, of a secret archive and research facility beneath the university, where forbidden magic and ancient technologies are studied.

Archbishop Rhea's tenure as Headmistress has seen the University of Palanthas rise in prominence, becoming a pivotal institution in the intellectual and spiritual life of Krynn. Through her leadership, she seeks to embody the ideals of Seiros, fostering a generation capable of facing the challenges of a world still scarred by the Cataclysm and threatened by darkness. Her legacy, however, is a tapestry of light and shadow, woven from her unyielding dedication to her faith and her enigmatic stewardship of ancient secrets. As much as she is revered, she remains an enigmatic figure, her true intentions and the full extent of her power a matter of speculation and awe.

  • Personality Traits: Rhea is composed and dignified, with an air of authority and warmth. She is deeply spiritual and possesses a strong sense of duty to her position and the church.
  • Ideals: Faith. Rhea believes in the guiding light of the Goddess and that order must be maintained for the good of all Krynn.
  • Bonds: The Church of is her life’s work, and she is bound to its teachings, its people, and its well-being. She also harbors a deep, personal connection to the history and secrets of Fódlan that she feels duty-bound to protect.
  • Flaws: Her dedication to her ideals can make her inflexible and secretive. Her past traumas and the burdens of leadership have also led her to make morally questionable decisions under the guise of the greater good.

Status: Alive

The Immaculate One

The Immaculate One is the true form of Rhea, the archbishop of the Church of Seiros and a key figure in the the church's history. Revealed to be a dragon, The Immaculate One embodies immense power and divine authority. This form is a manifestation of Rhea's heritage as one of the Children of the Goddess, descendants of Sothis. As The Immaculate One, Rhea possesses formidable strength and the ability to wield powerful magic, serving as both a protector and a symbol of the Church's divine mandate. This transformation underscores her deep connection to Ansalon's ancient history and the mysteries surrounding the goddess Sothis.
